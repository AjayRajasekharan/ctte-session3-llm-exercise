Program Overview:
This GenAI training program is designed to introduce undergraduate students to
Artificial Intelligence, Large Language Models (LLMs), and practical AI applications.

Course Structure:
The program is divided into five sessions conducted between October and February.

Session 1:
Introduction to AI, GenAI, and career opportunities in technology.
Overview of GitHub, development tools, and responsible AI usage.

Session 2:
Understanding Large Language Models (LLMs).
Prompt engineering basics and hands-on chatbot examples.

Session 3:
Building simple LLM-based chatbots using Python and Streamlit.
Introduction to model parameters and tuning.

Session 4:
Introduction to Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG).
Using documents to enhance chatbot responses.

Session 5:
Multi-agent systems, advanced demos, and capstone project showcase.

Outcome:
By the end of the program, students will build a GenAI project suitable for
interviews and portfolios.
